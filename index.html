About Us

A CPA Firm Built on Trust & Expertise

Kariuki Mugo & Associates is a Certified Public Accountants firm proudly based in Nakuru City, Kenya. We provide reliable accounting, tax compliance, audit, payroll, bookkeeping, and financial consulting services to businesses, organisations, and individuals across the Rift Valley and beyond. Our mission is to simplify financial management while keeping our clients fully compliant with KRA and Kenyan regulatory requirements.

ICPAK
Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Kenya

ICPAK is the professional body for certified accountants in Kenya, established under the Accountants Act (Cap. 531). Full membership signifies that our partners have met the highest standards of professional competence, ethics, and ongoing CPD requirements.

Full Member Active Status CPD Compliant
